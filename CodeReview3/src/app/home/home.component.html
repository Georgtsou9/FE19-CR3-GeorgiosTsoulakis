Why choose us

At Ristorante Il Gambero Rosso, we are serving up more than typical italian food.
In fact,Ristorante Il Gambero Rosso Famous recipe is one of our unexpected specialties.
Reminiscent of butcher shops back in the day, each slow-smoked, sizzling prime chop measures seven-fingers high.
Our signature recipe, that we have perfected for more than four decades,
is rubbed with a secret blend of seasonings,
cured and roasted on a rotisserie with pecan wood for up to six hours before it is topped with our signature herb-garlic butter,
then carved tableside.
